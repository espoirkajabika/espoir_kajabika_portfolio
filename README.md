# espoir-portfolio

## Description

A professional portfolio website showcasing my software engineering skills, projects, and experience. This site serves as a comprehensive platform to present my work, technical capabilities, and professional journey to potential employers, collaborators, and accelerator programs.

## Goal

This portfolio is designed for:
- **Recruiters** – to evaluate my technical skills and project experience
- **Collaborators** – to explore potential partnership opportunities
- **Accelerators** – to assess my capabilities for startup and innovation programs

## Planned Sections

- **About** – Introduction and professional background
- **Skills** – Technical expertise and competencies
- **Projects** – Featured work including:
  - FinShield – Financial security platform
  - PopupReach – Marketing engagement tool
  - Grant platform – Funding management system
  - Additional projects and contributions
- **Contact** – Ways to connect and reach out
